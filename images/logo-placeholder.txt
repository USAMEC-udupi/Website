LOGO PLACEHOLDER

Please place your logo image file here with one of these names:
- logo.png
- logo.jpg
- logo.svg
- logo.webp

The logo will be automatically displayed in:
1. Navigation bar
2. Browser tab (favicon)
3. Loading page
4. Footer

Recommended logo dimensions:
- Navigation: 48x48px or larger
- Favicon: 32x32px or 16x16px
- High resolution: 512x512px for various uses
