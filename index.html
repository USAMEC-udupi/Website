1950: The Humble Beginnings

Founded by Swamiji in a small village with just a thatched hut and a vision, Divine Light Matha began as a spiritual center offering free education to local children. The first students were taught under a banyan tree, with lessons combining traditional scriptures with modern subjects. This humble beginning laid the foundation for what would become a nationwide network of educational and spiritual institutions.
